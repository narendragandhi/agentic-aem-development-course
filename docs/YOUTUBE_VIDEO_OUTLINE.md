## Video Production Tips

### Recording Setup
- Use OBS (free) or Camtasia
- Screen + webcam overlay
- Microphone: Audio quality matters most

### Structure Per Video
1. Hook (30 sec) - What you'll learn
2. Concept (3-5 min) - Explain
3. Demo (5-10 min) - Show code
4. Summary (1 min) - Recap
5. CTA - Like, subscribe, next video

### Upload Schedule
- 1 video/week = 6 months to launch
- 2 videos/week = 3 months to launch
- Daily = 1 month to launch
